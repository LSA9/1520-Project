About
Gaggle

Tired of waiting in line at a so-called fast food joint or dealing with slow service at one of your favorite sit downs? Whether you live-to-eat or just simply eat-to-live the answer to both of these questions is most certainly yes. However, the wait for the web application to end your wait in long lines is over. Gaggle is a web-based application that uses user input in order to indicate the perceived busyness of many locations around you.
